Scenario 6

Child Welfare Agency and Prosecutor

START

Thirteen year-old Denise is arrested for assault on her mother. At the intake interview, Denise claims self-defense and talks about past instances in which her mother has hurt her.

NEXT

You are the district attorney, and you want information from the child welfare agency about Denise and her family to help you decide whether to file a petition against the youth, and also how to proceed if the case is filed (for example, whether to consent to an Adjournment Contemplating Dismissal).

Specifically, you want to ask:

  • Is the child welfare agency investigating the same incident?
  • If yes, what are/were the allegations?
  • What is the history of the family involvement with the agency?
  • Has the parent abused the youth in the past?
  • Is the family receiving services?
